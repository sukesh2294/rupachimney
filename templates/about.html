About Rupa Chimney

Leading the way in quality brick manufacturing since 1998

Our Story

Rupa Chimney was established in 1998 with a vision to provide high-quality construction materials to the growing infrastructure needs of Bihar and surrounding regions. What started as a small brick manufacturing unit has now grown into one of the most trusted names in the construction industry.

Located in Sikandra, Jamui, Bihar (811315), we have been serving contractors, builders, and individual homeowners with premium quality bricks and industrial chimneys for over two decades. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success.

We take pride in our traditional manufacturing techniques combined with modern quality control processes to deliver products that stand the test of time and weather conditions.

🏭 Our Mission

To provide high-quality, durable, and affordable construction materials that empower builders and homeowners to create strong, safe, and beautiful structures. We aim to contribute to the infrastructure development of our region while maintaining the highest standards of environmental responsibility.

👁️ Our Vision

To become the most trusted and preferred manufacturer of construction materials in Eastern India, known for our uncompromising quality, timely delivery, and exceptional customer service. We envision a future where every construction project in our region is built with Rupa Chimney products.
